About

Hi, my name is Richard Wilson, a JavaScript and Python Developer. Though I am pursuing a B.Sc in Computer Science right now, I had not decided on becoming a software developer at the begining of my degree program. Commiting myself to become a software developer has been one of the best decision I have made in my life. It has been an enlightening experience, ruthlessly exposing my weaknesses and faults. For this I can only be gratefull, as it has also shown me areas where I can improve myself as a person as well as a productive member of society. Most importantly it has rekindled my love of learning, solving problems and my dream of building something amazing. I love to challenge myself mentally and physically, so when I'm not programming I like to challenge my body in the gym. I believe that life is about balance, and balance brings longevity.
